Whipped Feta with Grilled Pita Bread
Yield: 3 cups
Purchase feta in whole blocks, bricks, or wedges as it stays fresh longer, it doesn’t dry out, and it gives
you more options as sometimes you wont to slice 0 thick slab of feta and other times you need large
crumbles. .
3 cups crumbled Greek feta
2/3 cup extra—virgin olive oil, plus more for serving
Zest and juice of 1 lemon
Freshly ground black pepper
1 teaspoon za'atar
Pitted Kalamata olives for serving
Warm pita bread for serving
To prepare the spread: Place feta, olive oil, lemon zest, juice, and a few grinds of black pepper
into the bowl of a food processor and blend until smooth and creamy.
To serve: Transfer the mixture into a serving bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with za'atar.
Scatter the dip with olives and serve with warm pita bread for dipping.
